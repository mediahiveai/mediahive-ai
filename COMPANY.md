## What Is MediaHive AI?

**MediaHive AI** (mediahive.ai) is an AI partner that helps businesses use artificial intelligence to:

- **Build websites & applications** — Framer Motion sites, landing pages, lean web apps, client portals
- **Design custom CRM systems** — bespoke pipelines and client management built for how you operate
- **Redesign & elevate brands** — website redesigns, UX refreshes, identity, and digital launches
- **Automate with AI** — custom workflows, agents, and platform integrations across your stack
- **Run social media workflows** — content pipelines, scheduling, and growth systems (not just posting)
- **Scale marketing agency delivery** — client onboarding, campaigns, creative, and reporting
- **Teach growth & agency building** — training, playbooks, and frameworks to grow your business
- **Drive organic growth** — SEO, content strategy, and search-optimised pages

We don't sell generic AI tools. We build **intelligence systems tailored to each client's business** — strategy first, then execution, then measurable impact.

## The Platform — Intelligence Field

MediaHive runs on a **4-layer intelligence field**. Every client deployment uses some or all of these layers:

| Layer | Tagline | What It Does | Products |
